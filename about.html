After graduating high school, I started my career as a Hair and Makeup Artist. I went from being a stylist, to a Salon Owner, to a Freelance Celebrity Stylist over a 10 year span. Some celebrities I've had the opportunity to groom/style are: Tia Mowry, Orlando Bloom, and Normani from 5th Harmony. After acheiving all of my childhood dreams, I decided to pursue my next love: Computer Technology. I received my Comptia A+ certification in August 2019. I then opened my own computer/mobile repair business. Two years later, I am now a Software Engineer. I'm excited and look forward to where this journey will take me. Thank you for your interest in my story!
